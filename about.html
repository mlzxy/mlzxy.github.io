I am Xinyu |zɪnjʊ| Zhang. I obtained B.E. in Electronic Engineering at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015. I received M.S. in Computer Science at University of California, San Diego (UCSD) in 2017, advised by Prof. Ken Kreutz-Delgado.

Unlike most of my Asian colleagues, who stayed in the US by going into the industry or pursuing a doctoral degree, I went back to China after graduating from UCSD for family reasons. At the start of my career, I worked in Horizon Robotics and Megvii, two AI startup companies. Currently, I am a software engineer at Microsoft Bing in Beijing.

Sadly, I used to have a broad interest in too many things, from hardware design, machine learning to frontend development. As my experience also reflects my confusion. However, in 2020, with the help of a senior friend, to whom I am genuinely graceful, I rethought my career.

I have kept dreaming of using AI to solve my favorite game, which I have played for over ten years (HOMM3 ). I find that there is a lot of knowledge behind and I plan to develop a research career. Meanwhile, I deeply realize that the foundation of machine learning research is mathematics, not just strokes of ideas. Therefore, I decided to move forward with a focused mentality and write blogs to track my path :D
